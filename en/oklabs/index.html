Code for Germany teams meet regularly in 13 cities. The teams are connected locally as well as globally, get input und support from mentors, and work colaboratively on code and applications. Every lab consits of members and lab leads. The leads are oranizing the meetings, and are the contact persons to the rest of the network. As of January 2015 5 additiona labs joined Code for Germany and become part of the network.
